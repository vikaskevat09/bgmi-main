Accounts

Privacy Policy

We collect only the information needed to deliver your order and run the service: your name, email, phone number, the in-game IDs you enter, and your order history.

Your data is used to process orders, deliver top-ups, provide support, and send order confirmations. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Payment data

Your rights

You can request access to or deletion of your personal data by contacting support.

Refund Policy

Because in-game currency and voucher codes are delivered instantly and cannot be returned, all sales are generally final once delivered.

When refunds apply

If a payment is charged but the top-up is not delivered due to a fault on our side, we will either re-deliver the order or issue a full refund to the original payment method. Refund requests must be raised within 7 days of the order with your order ID.

Orders delivered to an incorrect ID entered by the customer are not eligible for a refund, since the currency has already been delivered.
